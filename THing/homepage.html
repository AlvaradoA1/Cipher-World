Welcome to CipherWorld, here you will have the opportunity to engage your critical and logical thinking skills. This will be an excellent introduction to the science of cryptography through a simpler subsection called cryptograms.

What exactly is this all about?

In the world of secret messages, there are 3 main categories which can fall into: Cryptography: the study of protecting/creating communications through the use of codes, so that only intended recipients can read and understand such messages.

Cryptology: the study of codes, in both encryption and decryption.

Cryptanalysis: the study of solving such coded messages, or breaking codes, when you are not the intended recipient You will be playing the role of Cryptanalyst.

In this website, we will feature cryptograms, which are short puzzles that consist of a few encrypted, or encoded, phrases. There are simple substitution ciphers where letters are replaced by a different letter according to a randomized key. In order to solve the puzzle, cryptanalysts must recover the original letters of the encrypted message.
